MISSION

Our mission is to create an awesome online shopping hub that introduces folks to the amazing eco-friendly goodies from "Papemelroti" while also shining a spotlight on our local heroes - our homegrown brands. We're all about spreading the word on the awesome sustainability vibe of "papemelroti" and making it super easy for you to support our talented Filipino artisans and businesses. Come join us in building a community that's not just about shopping, but also about caring for our planet and celebrating the incredible creativity of our local scene. Let's shop with purpose and make a difference, one eco-friendly purchase at a time!

VISION

Our vision is to redefine online shopping by becoming the ultimate destination for eco-conscious consumers, where they can discover the eco-friendly wonders of "papemelroti" and uplift local brands with every click. We envision a virtual marketplace brimming with sustainable treasures, where each purchase serves as a vote for a greener planet and a stronger local economy. By fostering a community of mindful shoppers, we aspire to inspire widespread environmental stewardship and ignite a movement towards a more sustainable and socially responsible way of living. Together, let's envision a future where shopping isn't just about acquiring goods but also about making a positive impact on the world around us.
